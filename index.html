Teddy Bear News

The Teddy Bear is eager to please and learn. The Teddy Bear is generally easy to train; they do very well at picking up commands and tricks. However, like many smaller dogs, the Teddy Bear can be difficult to housebreak. Lots of praise and treats as well as a consistent routine can help them along.
